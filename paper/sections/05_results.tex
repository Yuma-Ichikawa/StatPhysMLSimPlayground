\subsection{Pre-registered hypotheses}

Five central hypotheses organize interpretation. First, positional and semantic recovery
separate only after sufficient architecture and data complexity. Second, head
specialization is controlled by H/r rather than raw H. Third, attention and MLP
interventions reveal distinct algorithmic phases. Fourth, glassy replica structure appears
only when computational and statistical thresholds separate. Fifth, common coordinates
continue across diffusion, RL, and collective reasoning without requiring equal exponents.
Each is rejected if its registered size, response, and oracle diagnostics fail.
