\subsection{What breaks and what can be repaired}

The claim ledger distinguishes a preregistered augmented surrogate from a
macrovariable selected after failure. Holdout A can diagnose that the base
model omits specialization, nonlocality, strategy support, or intrinsic field.
Selection occurs on calibration data; improvement on disjoint Holdout B is the
repair test. The same points are never used both to invent and validate a
macrovariable.
